Privacy

Privacy Notice

This site uses a protected server-side contact handler for booking inquiries. It does not publish the destination email address in page source.

  • The booking form submits to a same-origin backend endpoint.
  • Contact form submissions are used only to respond to the inquiry.
  • No client-side secrets are embedded in the site.
  • No advertising or tracking pixels are loaded by default.

For booking inquiries or privacy questions, use the contact form on the homepage.
