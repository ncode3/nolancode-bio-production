Bio

Nolan is the founder and executive director of the Atlanta AI & Robotics Initiative, a hands-on workforce initiative building AI infrastructure, robotics, quantum, and data center talent pipelines through Morehouse College and the Atlanta University Center. He helps students, executives, and institutions understand the systems underneath modern AI: energy, chips, infrastructure, models, edge deployment, and real-world adoption.
